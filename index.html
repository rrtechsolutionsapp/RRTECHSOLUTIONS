02A  Honest doubt

“WhatsApp se kaam chal raha hai. App kyun?”

Bilkul sahi sawaal. WhatsApp important hai, aur rahega. App WhatsApp ko replace nahi karta — app aapka permanent rate-board, catalogue aur offer counter banata hai, jo customer ke phone mein hamesha pada rehta hai.

WhatsApp tab kaam karta hai jab customer aapse baat shuru kare. App tab kaam karta hai jab customer ne abhi baat shuru bhi nahi ki.

Jewellery mein sale aksar “aaj rate kya hai?” ya “koi design dekha tha” se shuru hoti hai. App wahi first thought aapki shop ke naam se connect karta hai.

Only WhatsApp

  • Customer ko pehle message karna padta hai.
  • Rate aur offer chat mein neeche dab jaate hain.
  • New designs sirf selected logon ko forward hote hain.
  • Staff busy ho toh reply late hota hai.

App + WhatsApp

  • Customer khud rate check kar leta hai.
  • Offer, catalogue aur shop identity ek jagah rehti hai.
  • Push notification se festival ya new arrival seedha dikhta hai.
  • Interested customer app se call/WhatsApp par aa jaata hai.

Whatever you type into the demo form stays in your own browser. When you press Send on WhatsApp or Send by email, your own WhatsApp or Gmail opens with the message already written. Nothing reaches us until you press send there. This site has no server that stores form entries.

What we receive when you contact us

If you choose to send that message or call us, we receive what you sent — typically your shop name, your phone number and your requirement. We use it only to reply to you, prepare your demo and provide support. We do not sell it and we do not share it with anyone outside RR Tech Solutions.

Cookies and advertising

We use Google Ads conversion tracking to see which advertisements lead to genuine enquiries. It records that a visit resulted in a WhatsApp tap or a form send — it does not tell us who you are. Google may set cookies for this. You can block cookies in your browser settings, or opt out at adssettings.google.com. This page also loads fonts from Google Fonts.

How long we keep it

Enquiry messages stay in our WhatsApp and email like any normal business conversation. Ask us any time to delete your details and we will remove them from our side.
